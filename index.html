Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crytals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your total number exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden to you until you click on it

Each time when the game starts, the value of the crytals change.
